Life

Utkirbek grew up in one of the small towns of Andijan where he pursued his early studies at school with distinction in foreign languages. During his time in town, Utkirbek attended several language courses and learned to flourish as a man with modern professions. Courses helped him to research more about the world and lifestyle in trend. With another word, that was the early step to be active socially and professionally.

His ambitions led him to study modern professions in one of the leading universities in the capital city.

Utkirbek is now recognized as a Business Consultant majored in Management and Marketing. Apart from that he mastered the skills of interpretation, copyrighting, screenplay writing, acting and modeling while he was asstisting the marketing department of MilliyTV, one of the top 3 TV channels of Uzbekistan during his studies for bachelor's degree.

It was a great chance for him to be in a media sphere to shape his hobbies as professional skills while majoring a different pathway at university.

Academic

My academic career is rooted in Tashkent city where I attended the Academic Lyceum of the University of World Economy and Diplomacy for three years. In 2018 I graduated at the top of my class meriting a course in Foreign Philology with specific of English Language.

After successfully finished the lyceum, at the same year, I started my bachelor's degree in Business Administration with a pathway of Management and Marketing at Westminster International University in Tashkent.
